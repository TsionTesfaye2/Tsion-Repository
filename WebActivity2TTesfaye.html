1.Place the toast in the toaster.Leave the toast in the toaster for three minutes.

2.Spread butter on the toasted bread.











3.Grab a ratio of one part sugar.

4.Grab a ratio of three parts cinnamon.

5.Stir the cinnamon and sugar within the bowl.

6.You should continue to stir this concoction until it has a smooth brown undertone.

7.Place the stirred cinnamon and sugar onto the toast.





8.Sit down and enjoy your meal.


9.Lastly, share this new breakfast meal with your family!
